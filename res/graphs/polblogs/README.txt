A web network whose vertices represent political blogs, and edges represent
hyperlinks between the blogs: 636 liberal and 586 conservative blogs.

Information
- fully-connected and undirected
- 1,222 vertices
- 16,714 edges
- 2 labels
- 586 vertices of label 0
- 636 vertices of label 1
- no unlabeled vertices
